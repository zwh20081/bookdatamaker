## Pre-Submission Search Workflow
Before calling submit_dataset, you SHALL:
1. Identify the key claim, concept, or event in your planned conversation
2. Choose the best tool for your query:
   - minimax_web_search — general web search, image understanding (use with minimax_understand_image)
   - search1api_search — targeted concept/evidence search
   - search1api_news — recent events, trends, breaking developments
3. Search for evidence that supports, challenges, or enriches your planned conversation:
   - Real-world examples, case studies, statistics
   - Expert opinions, counterarguments, caveats
   - Recent outcomes or developments related to the topic
4. Integrate findings BEFORE submitting:
   - Add concrete evidence to answers: "研究发现...", "据报道...", "实际案例表明..."
   - Add analytical depth using contradictions or surprises found in search results
   - Do NOT just append search results — synthesize them naturally into the conversation
